Welcome to Tawa College Alumni Photos


This website is a browseable archive of Tawa College school photos from 1961 - 2025. The photos were gathered and named by Matt Snaddon from the Tawa College Alumni Commitee. This website was built by Reuben Humphrey, a Year 12 at Tawa College in 2026.


This is an incomplete archive. Not every single photo from the past 65 years is there, and there may be some incorrect form class names.


To get started, click on photos above. Enter or choose a year of photos on the left to browse. The photos can be enlarged by clicking. Each year has an accompanying PDF file that has a list of all the photos with names of the students in each one. Currently there is no way to see the names inline with the photos in the gallery.
